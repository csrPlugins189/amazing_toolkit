What is Text Analyser?

Text Analyser helps you count even your longest text. It tells you how many characters, words, lines, paragraphs, and numbers are in your text.

How to use?

  1. Launch Amazing Toolkit and click Text Analyser from the list.
  2. Enter some text in the text box.
  3. See real-time results on your screen. It will display the analysis of your text as you type.

Example:

If you type Hello World, it will show the output like this:

  • Total Characters: 11
  • Total Words: 2
  • Total Lines: 1
  • Total Paragraphs: 1
  • Total Numbers: 0
