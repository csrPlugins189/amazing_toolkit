Introduction to Amazing Toolkit:

Amazing Toolkit is an Android application designed to enhance the lives of visually impaired people by boosting their productivity.
This application is developed by Abd-ur-Rehman Mughal.
It was released on Saturday, June 7th, 2025.
Fully compatible with Android 7 and higher!
It is completely free to use.

Features Include:

  1. Text Analyser
  2. Text Reverser
